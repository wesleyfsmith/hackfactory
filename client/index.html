Multi-Player Educational Space Simulation

Classrooms were retrofitted to look like the inside of a space ship. Imagine being on the bridge of the star trek voyager while you explore the stars. Over 300,000 students have gone through these simulations

  • Java
  • Swing
  • Ogre3D
  • Tensor Flow

Live Sound

A scheduling app for local bands

Live sound is an web based application that allows to grow in multiple ways. The app is first and foremost built to allow local small businesses to easily schedule out a band/preformer to come play for them in a single instance, or in a reocurring fashion. It also allows bands to find local musicians who would are looking to join a band. This app is looking to revolutionize the local music industy, giving great musiscians a chance to shine.

Technologies Used:

  • Ruby
  • Ruby on Rails
  • Javascript
  • React.js
  • Jquery

HipGeo

A blogging and social networking app for travel.

HipGeo was a blogging and social networking app for travel. Users are able to use the app to passively track your route on trips and intelligently collect your media and text uploads into replayable trip posts. The social elements let you see what's going on around you as well as keep you up to date on your friends' adventures. HackFactory developed the iPhone and Android apps along with a fully exensible cloud API. HipGeo was featured in Forbes, TechCrunch, PCMag, ReadWriteWeb, Engadget, and GigaOM.

Technologies Used:

  • Android SDK
  • PhP
  • Amazon Web Services
  • Android NDK
  • FFMPEG

RapiDoc

Peer to Peer Payment for Medical Services.

RapiDoc allows patients with HSA accounts to pay their doctors directly for medical services. HackFactory developed the backend as well as the mobile application for the system. We had to build a web front end for doctors to use, as well as integrate with their current scheduling API. We used stripe to handle payments through the platform. Rapidoc is currently in a private beta with a network of 100 doctors.

Technologies Used:

  • Stripe
  • Javascript
  • Cordova
  • Mongo DB
  • NodeJS
